# Privacy Policy for FB Hide Recommendations and Reels

**Last Updated:** November 29, 2024

## Overview
FB Hide Recommendations and Reels is a Chrome extension designed to help users customize their Facebook feed by hiding unwanted content types such as Stories, Reels, Suggestions, and Games.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

### What We DON'T Collect:
- ❌ No personally identifiable information
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No Facebook credentials
- ❌ No user activity or behavior data
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No cookies

## How the Extension Works
All content filtering happens **locally on your device**:
1. The extension reads Facebook's page structure in real-time
2. Applies hiding rules based on your preferences
3. All settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
4. No data leaves your browser

## Permissions Used

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** Save your configuration preferences locally
- **Data Stored:** Your settings for what content to hide (e.g., Stories: ON, Reels: ON)
- **Location:** Chrome's local storage on your device only

### Host Permissions (facebook.com)
- **Purpose:** Access Facebook pages to detect and hide content
- **How Used:** Read page structure and apply CSS/DOM modifications
- **Data Sent:** None - all processing is local

## Third-Party Services
We do **NOT** use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Data Security
Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or transmit.
